    Pappaya Cloud

    Pappaya Cloud aims to take your organization on a digital transformation to create a centralized IT infrastructure. With global tier 4 data centers, we offer a host of services - scalable computing resources, highly efficient storage solutions at 30% lesser cost. Pappaya Cloud partners with some top brands in the industry, including Oracle, Dell, Microsoft, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, Vmware, among others.

    Pappaya Sign

    Pappaya Sign is an intuitive, online signing platform that allows businesses to sign anytime anywhere. Compliant with EU Regulation 910/2014 (eIDAS) and US ESIGN Act, Pappaya Sign reduces 90% in processing errors and saves 90% of shipping costs. With premium support and secure identification protocol, Pappaya Sign is available in 4 different packages.

    Pappaya ERP

    Experienced in building digital classrooms, Pappaya ERP has revolutionized the digital learning platform in school globally. Our ERP system includes various features, including a visitor management system, student-faculty performance analysis, chatbots, VR classroom, AI-based tutoring, and more. Pappaya ERP is being used across 80,000+ schools across the globe.

    About us

    Pappaya Systems

    Pappaya Systems is an amalgamation of hardware and software offerings that provides end-to-end technical support to schools and other educational institutions globally, including Dubai, the UK, and India. Supporting the virtual classroom system, Pappaya offers the basket of services needed. The offerings include Pappaya Studio, Pappaya Wide-angle viewer, Pappaya Cloud, Pappaya Smart TV, Pappaya SmartTrack, and PTM that supports parent-teacher meetings.

    About us

    Pappaya Financial Services

    Pappaya Financial services is a robust remittance/money transfer software that supports banking and cross-border money transfer operations. Built on a highly modular component-based architecture, our platform offers a complete ecosystem to move money securely worldwide. The software is adaptable to the market’s ever-evolving demands offering easy implementation for both stand-alone or gradual deployment, thus maximizing return-on-investment.

    About us

    Edumarket

    Edumarket is a one-of-a-kind online platform exclusively for educational products. The ideology is to bridge the gap between sellers and purchasers through its extensive network to reach students, schools, and parents directly right to their doorstep. Removing the middleman in the process of identifying products, Edumarket is user-friendly and allows parents to purchase from reliable sources, and allows sellers to target their products to the right audience.
